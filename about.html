About

Energy storage, explained clearly.

Batteries and Beyond covers what's actually happening in energy storage — from cell chemistry and electrode design to the manufacturing steps that turn raw materials into working cells.

The goal is simple: cut through hype and marketing, and explain the engineering as it really works — for engineers, researchers, and anyone building in energy storage who wants substance over headlines.

Batteries and Beyond is independently run. Content is written and curated to be accurate and useful first, not to promote any single company, product, or chemistry.
